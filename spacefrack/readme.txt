SpaceFrack v1.0

You are the pilot of the latest in Super-Expensive Outer-Space Fracking Technology! 
Drive around to find resources and frack them open. 
But beware - rival mining companies have been drilling these parts for years and won't hesitate to blast you away.

Game engine: Atom.js
